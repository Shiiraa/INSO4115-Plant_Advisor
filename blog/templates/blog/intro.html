ABOUT US

What is 'My travel Record'?

Let’s face it, everyone loves to travel. Travel takes us out of our comfort zones and inspires us to see, taste and try new things. It constantly challenges us, not only to adapt to and explore new surroundings, but also to engage with different people, to embrace adventures as they come and to share new and meaningful experiences with friends and loved ones. Sometimes, people want to keep a record of all the wonderful places they had previously visited and most importantly, to be able to share this information and experiences with friends and family through the internet along with experience, pictures, reviews, etc. Currently, there is no efficient and reliable way to do this through the internet.

Since there is no available platform that has such capabilities and efficiency, people have nothing to rely on. There are some platforms that have some level of capabilities but none have the efficiency and clarity that many people are looking for. No platform displays in a map all locations a user has previously visited. Meta, Instagram and Twitter have tried it. They offer a guide or tutorial on how to use their services because its not clear enough. And most importantly, they all lack a better UI with crystal clear layout and button identification so that users know what they want to do intuitively.

The main focus of this website is the people, who want to share their travel experience, picture, ratings, social events, expectations, tours, etc. with their friends and family through the internet. Users will be able to share their profile by simply giving others their user profile url that will lead others to their profile. Users will also have the option to edit their profile status such as private or public. Account editing is also a feature to be implemented in order to create user-profile uniqueness and distinction. Posts and pictures will be applied by users and stored in the platform database and displayed to the world. Users will have the ability to update their profile by adding if they are traveling. This can indicate if they are currently traveling to a certain destination. To be even more specific, they can add country, continent, hotel and activity for others to view. At the end of their trip they can post their experience.
